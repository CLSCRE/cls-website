From $2M bridge loans to $35M multifamily refinances — three recent deals that show our range.

Real Deals We Have Closed

Every deal is different — different property, different market, different borrower goals. Here are three recent transactions that illustrate the range of what we do and how we structure creative solutions.

Multifamily Refinance — Southern California

Loan Amount: $35,000,000
Property: 200+ unit apartment community
Lender: A life insurance company
Terms: 10-year fixed, 30-year amortization, 65% LTV
The sponsor needed to refinance a maturing CMBS loan with better terms and flexibility. We sourced a life company execution that reduced the rate by 85 bps and eliminated the lockout period in favor of a declining prepayment schedule.

Industrial Acquisition — Phoenix Metro

Loan Amount: $12,500,000
Property: 80,000 SF distribution warehouse
Lender: A regional bank
Terms: 7-year fixed, 25-year amortization, 70% LTV
A first-time industrial buyer needed competitive terms on a tight 45-day close. We identified a regional bank with strong appetite for Phoenix industrial and closed on schedule with rate lock at application.

Bridge-to-Permanent — Los Angeles

Loan Amount: $2,800,000
Property: Mixed-use retail/office, value-add
Lender: A private bridge lender (acquisition), a credit union (permanent)
Terms: 12-month bridge at 75% LTC, refinanced to 5-year fixed at 70% LTV
The buyer needed to close quickly on an off-market deal before stabilization. We structured a two-phase strategy: a bridge loan for acquisition and renovation, then a permanent takeout once occupancy reached 90%.
