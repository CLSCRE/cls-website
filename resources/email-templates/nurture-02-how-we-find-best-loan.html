How We Find You the Best Loan

When you go directly to a bank or lender, you get one option — whatever that institution happens to offer. When you work with us, you get access to the entire capital markets landscape, and we do the work of finding the best fit.

Here is how our process works:

1

Understand Your Deal

We start with the fundamentals: property type, location, loan amount, timeline, and your specific goals — lowest rate, maximum proceeds, flexible prepayment, or speed of execution.

2

Match Against 1,000+ Lenders

We filter our lender database by property type, geography, loan size, and current appetite. Every lender has different sweet spots — we know who is actively lending on what, right now.

3

Competitive Bidding

We present your deal to multiple qualified lenders simultaneously. Competition between lenders consistently produces better rates, higher proceeds, and more favorable terms than a single-lender approach.

4

Present Options and Close

We give you a clear comparison of your best options with our recommendation, then manage the process through closing — lender communication, document coordination, and timeline management.

Our lender network includes: Life insurance companies, national and regional banks, credit unions, CMBS lenders, agency lenders (Fannie, Freddie, FHA), CDFIs, debt funds, and private capital sources.
